Cat Bluemke

Cat Bluemke makes games, performances, and immersive experiences. Her site-responsive projects intervene in technological narratives of Canadian landscape, labour, and infrastructure. She is Executive Director of Centre for Art Tapes in Halifax, where she is building resources for experimental games and interactive media.
